# Midnight Commander

## Hotkeys

- Quick cd: [[Alt+c]]
- Quick search in panels: [[Alt+s]] or [[Ctrl+s]]
- Swap panels: [[Ctrl+u]]
- Show dir size: [[Ctrl+Space]]
- Find file: [[Alt+?]] (on laptop keyboard: [[Alt+Shift]] [[?]])
- Toggle dir hotlist: [[Ctrl+\]]
- Screen list: [[Alt+`]]
- Next screen: [[Alt+‚Üê]]
- Show hidden files: [[Alt+.]]
